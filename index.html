About Me

Hi, my name is Jay-Em and I'm a 20-year-old guy currently residing in Angeles City, Pampanga. I have a passion for capturing the beauty of nature through photography, especially the moon, stars, and other breathtaking sceneries.

Aside from photography, I also love to read books and immerse myself in anime and Korean dramas. It's always a great way for me to unwind after a long day and let my imagination run wild.

Growing up, I've always been fascinated by the wonders of the universe and the mysteries of life. It's what drives me to pursue my passion and capture these moments through my lens. I believe that every picture tells a story, and through my photography, I hope to share my story with the world.

Thanks for taking the time to get to know a little bit about me. I'm excited to see where life takes me and what other adventures I can capture through my lens.
